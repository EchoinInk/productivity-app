## Rules

- Reduce decisions
- Reduce taps
- Reduce visual clutter
- Prioritize Today view
- Avoid punishment mechanics
- Support imperfect consistency
- Make logging fast
- Prefer guided flows
- Use progressive disclosure

## UX Goals

Users should be able to:
- log actions in under 5 seconds
- understand screens instantly
- recover from missed days easily
- avoid overwhelm

## Avoid

- giant dashboards
- dense tables
- excessive filters
- over-complicated settings
- shame-based streak systems
