Lumo is an ADHD-friendly personal operating system focused on reducing cognitive overload through fast, forgiving, low-friction workflows.

Core principles:
- low shame
- low friction
- visual calmness
- quick logging
- progressive disclosure
- mobile-first interactions

The app should feel like:
- calm
- supportive
- simple
- fast

NOT:
- spreadsheet-like
- overwhelming
- data-heavy
- corporate
