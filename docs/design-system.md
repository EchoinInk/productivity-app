# Design System

## Typography
- Title: bold, large
- Subtitle: muted
- Body: standard

---

## Spacing
- 4, 8, 12, 16, 24 scale

---

## Cards
- rounded-xl / rounded-2xl
- subtle shadow
- no heavy borders

---

## Icons
- 32–40px
- no background
- aligned right or inline

---

## Buttons
- rounded-full
- active:scale-[0.97]
- gradient for primary actions
