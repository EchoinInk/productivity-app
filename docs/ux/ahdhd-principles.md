# ADHD UX Principles

## Goals

Reduce:
- cognitive overload
- friction
- shame
- excessive decision-making

Support:
- imperfect consistency
- quick logging
- visual calmness
- progressive disclosure
- guided workflows

## Rules

- prioritize Today view
- minimize taps
- avoid clutter
- avoid dense dashboards
- avoid punishment mechanics
- optimize for low energy states

## Product Feeling

The app should feel:
- calm
- supportive
- forgiving
- lightweight

NOT:
- corporate
- analytical
- overwhelming
- spreadsheet-like
