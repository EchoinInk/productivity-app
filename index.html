WHY LUMO EXISTS

For the days when “just be organised” feels impossible.

Lumo is for the version of you who is trying — with kids, mahi, brain fog, neurodivergence, grief, or just the quiet exhaustion of being alive right now. It doesn’t demand a morning routine, a perfect system, or a colour‑coded life. It simply gives you a soft place to land and a tiny bit of structure when everything else feels like too much.

WHO IT’S FOR

The not‑quite‑together humans.

If you’ve ever bounced off “productivity” apps because they made you feel worse, not better — Lumo is for you.

  • Neurodivergent brains
  • Parents in the thick of it
  • Burnt‑out professionals
  • Soft‑hearted overthinkers
  • People who keep “starting again on Monday”

HOW IT FEELS

Lumo is less “optimise every second” and more “let’s gently remember the few things that actually matter today.” It’s a tiny glow in the corner of your life, not a spotlight on everything you’re not doing.

HOW IT WORKS

Tiny steps, soft structure.

  1. Capture the few things that actually matter today — not a 47‑item backlog, just the gentle essentials.
  2. Let Lumo hold them for you, with soft visual structure that doesn’t shout when you fall behind.
  3. Come back when you can. No streaks broken, no guilt, no “you’ve missed 9 days” banners. Just a quiet, steady glow.
